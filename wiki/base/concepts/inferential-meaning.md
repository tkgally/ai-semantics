# Inferential meaning

**Stub.**

Meaning as inferential role: an expression's meaning is what it licenses you to infer to and from. Brandom's inferentialism is the systematic statement; conceptual-role semantics in philosophy of mind is the broader family.

For the LLM case: Piantadosi & Hill (2022) argue explicitly for "meaning without reference" — that meaningful expressions need not bottom out in extra-linguistic anchoring, that inferential role is enough. This is the strongest pro-LLM-meaning position in current literature.

Operationalizes naturally through entailment / NLI behavior, behavior under inference-licensing constructions, and systematic substitution that preserves or breaks inferential role.
