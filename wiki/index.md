- [`base/concepts/constructional-meaning.md`](base/concepts/constructional-meaning.md) — CxG form–meaning pairing; underwrites the `constructional` tag. Grounded in Weissweiler 2023. (filled 2026-05-29)
- [`base/concepts/coercion.md`](base/concepts/coercion.md) — constructional coercion: the construction overriding the verb (caused-motion, *way*); the sharpest Tier-4 wedge; the project's own add-vs-cancel asymmetry observation. Sharpens `constructional-meaning`; grounds the four argument-structure result pages. (created 2026-05-29)
- [`base/concepts/relational-meaning.md`](base/concepts/relational-meaning.md) — meaning constituted *between* agents (the charter's distinctive, under-explored second axis); the "second ladder" with trajectory-dependence (live-vs-shuffled) as candidate bottom rung; anchor ratified-but-unfetched. (created 2026-05-29)
- [`base/concepts/polysemy.md`](base/concepts/polysemy.md) — polysemy (graded, related senses) vs. homonymy (discrete, unrelated); sense as Fregean `Sinn` at fine grain; the base-concept home for the project's **lexical wedge**. Refines `referential-meaning`; the polysemy/homonymy split is the wedge's built-in internal control; distributional context-similarity is the null. Canonical lexicographic sources (Cruse/Murphy/Lyons) still in `wanted.md`. (created 2026-05-29)
- [`base/concepts/multimodal-compositionality.md`](base/concepts/multimodal-compositionality.md) — the VLM-compositionality question: do vision-language models compose lexical/relational meaning across modalities or behave like *bags-of-words*? The cross-modal analogue of the constructional wedge; a base-layer home for `grounded.perceptual`. Flags that the cited benchmarks are *discriminative* (image-text matching), unlike the project's *generative* panel. Grounded in Winoground + ARO/SugarCrepe. (created 2026-05-30; **multimodal axis**)
- [`base/concepts/symbol-grounding-problem.md`](base/concepts/symbol-grounding-problem.md) — Harnad's symbol-grounding problem: the symbol→sensorimotor *anchoring mechanism* (distinct from `grounding.md`'s binary-vs-gradual debate, which it refines); the philosophical ancestor of Bender-Koller form-vs-meaning. Grounded in Harnad 1990 + Barsalou 1999. (created 2026-05-30; **multimodal axis**)
- [`base/concepts/embodied-cognition.md`](base/concepts/embodied-cognition.md) — sensorimotor/simulation theories of meaning (Barsalou perceptual symbols) and **where they make testable contact with model behaviour** (honest about what is *not* testable on a VLM); carries the still-unanchored `referential.externalist` (Putnam/Evans) gap as embodiment's philosophical cousin. (created 2026-05-30; **multimodal axis**)
