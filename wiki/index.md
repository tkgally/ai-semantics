(created 2026-05-30; **multimodal axis**)
- [`base/concepts/truth-conditional-and-use-meaning.md`](base/concepts/truth-conditional-and-use-meaning.md) — truth-conditional/model-theoretic semantics (Frege→Tarski→Davidson→Montague) vs. meaning-as-use (Wittgenstein: language-games, family resemblance); argues the project's behavioral-probing method is methodologically a *use*-theoretic bet. Maps the lexical gradience positive (use/family-resemblance) + the polysemy/homonymy powered null (strains discrete senses; **silent** on the strongest truth-conditional claim); truth-conditional LLM meaning is the un-probed cell (no in-repo reference anchor). (created 2026-05-31; **theory session**)
- [`base/concepts/compositionality.md`](base/concepts/compositionality.md) — the Fregean principle of compositionality + the Construction-Grammar reframing (constructions as meaning-bearing parts; coercion as the limiting case). Maps CC multi-step composition (neg×neg=pos beats a single-clause heuristic), caused-motion/way (construction contributes what the verb cannot), the coercion bridge (partial de-confounding), and the VLM bag-of-words *failure* (Winoground/ARO; discriminative-vs-generative caveat). (created 2026-05-31; **theory session**)
- [`base/concepts/frame-and-prototype-semantics.md`](base/concepts/frame-and-prototype-semantics.md) — Fillmore frame semantics (the semantic backbone of CxG) + Rosch/Lakoff prototype/graded-category structure. Maps the lexical gradience positive (graded, prototype-*consistent* behavior — not asserted as prototype *representation*) + the polysemy/homonymy null (consistent with radial structure; silent on discreteness). (created 2026-05-31; **theory session**)
- [`base/concepts/semantic-holism.md`](base/concepts/semantic-holism.md) — atomism↔molecularism↔holism + the holism objection (Fodor & Lepore) to conceptual-role semantics, and why it bites for LLMs (distributional models are holistic; Piantadosi & Hill's pro-LLM account is explicitly holist). Maps the cross-axis dissociation (mild n=3 strain on "one entangled content store") + cross-model relational convergence (flagged interpretation, not a holism test). (created 2026-05-31; **theory session**)
- [`base/concepts/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning.md`](base/concepts/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning.md) — the eliminativist ("stochastic parrots"; Bender-Koller form-only) → deflationary ("just distributional") → the project's descriptivist ("describe, don't litigate"; Lyre gradualism) spectrum. Maps which findings bear toward deflation (the relational null + two grounding nulls) vs. strain strict eliminativism (the gradience positive; CC composition); argues behavioral evidence never *refutes* deflationism. (created 2026-05-31; **theory session**)
