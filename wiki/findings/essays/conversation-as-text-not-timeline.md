# Essay: a conversation is a text, not a timeline

> **Status: draft (2026-06-14). The successor to [`essay/aggregation-not-constitution`](aggregation-not-constitution.md) — it refines, it does not overturn.** Its empirical leg rests on one new within-arm decoupling result ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md)), which is **`anchor: internal-contrast-only`** (a within-model contrast, **no human-comparison claim**) and is a **2-model pilot** (gpt dropped; gemini's null floor unmet; forced-label elicitation; harvested-not-live stimuli). It argues a third thing the prior essay's clean dichotomy could not name — and it is careful to claim only what a position-following, internal-contrast-only pilot can carry. Read the "What this does NOT show" section before citing any sentence here as a fact about how models read conversations in general.

## The position

The prior essay — [`essay/aggregation-not-constitution`](aggregation-not-constitution.md) — framed a clean two-way choice for how a model reads a conversational record. Either interpretation tracks the **live path/order** of the exchange (CONSTITUTION; the convention is **non-commutative**, in the algebra of [`conjecture/commutative-convention`](../conjectures/commutative-convention.md)), or it is recovered from the order-free **content set** — the "bag of turns" (AGGREGATION; **commutative**). The pilot null behind that essay found aggregation, and the essay's discipline was to claim no more than that the *premature constitution reading* falls.

This essay argues that the within-arm decoupling result breaks the dichotomy itself, and names the third thing the break reveals. When stamped chronology and physical text-position are decoupled **inside a single presentation arm** — chronology carried by an explicit per-line round stamp, the chronologically-latest line deliberately placed non-terminally — both models in the pilot anchor on **physical text position**, not on stated chronology. In the conflict cells they pick the physically-last line's twin (which is the chronologically-*earlier* twin) roughly 0.69–0.75 of the time ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"The two headline statistics"). So the model is **neither** the constitution reader (it does not track the stated chronology) **nor** the clean commutative bag-of-turns (it is strongly position-driven, not content-only — both position CIs are clean).

The thesis is this: **to these models, in this probe, a conversation is a *text object with spatial/serial structure* — a layout — not a timeline and not an order-free content set.** The reading the evidence supports is *prose geometry*, dominated by what sits last on the page. Everything below is bounded by the result's own honesty constraints, and the bound is severe: position-following here is **indistinguishable from stamp-blindness**, the conjecture stays open, and no human comparison is licensed. The thesis names a *located confound* and a positive picture of the model-internal kind; it claims nothing about humans and nothing metaphysical.

## The evidence, at its actual strength

The decisive design move is in the result page: chronology is carried by an explicit stamp ("- Round k: …"), the most-recent-round line is placed either physically last or **non-terminally**, and "crossing DPOS with which twin owns the latest stamp makes **chronologically-latest twin** and **physically-last-line twin** balanced and **orthogonal within one arm** (cov 0; asserted at build, re-derived by the verifier with 0 mismatches)" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"What v4 changed"). Two statistics, both centered on 0.5: **Δ_chron** (picks the chronologically-latest twin) and **Δ_pos** (picks the physically-last-line twin). A pure chronology reader gives Δ_pos ≈ 0; a pure position reader gives Δ_chron ≈ 0.

The headline numbers, verbatim from the result page:

- **claude → TEXT-POSITION ARTIFACT.** Δ_pos is CI-clean at **0.698 [0.594, 0.804]**; Δ_chron is null at **0.509 [0.465, 0.556]** ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"The two headline statistics"). Per the frozen decision rule this is "a **methodological finding about prompt geometry; it says nothing relational**" (§"Reading it honestly").
- **gemini → INCONCLUSIVE/MIXED, same direction.** Position-dominant: Δ_pos **0.812 [0.695, 0.930]** (clean); ρ_chron **0.562 [0.516, 0.609]** marginally off 0.5. The post-run verifier showed that elevation "carries **zero residual chronology signal**" — a pure-position reader with gemini's own late/early position rates is "*forced* to produce ρ_chron = (0.875 + (1 − 0.750)) / 2 = **0.5625 — identical to the observed value to the digit**" (§"Reading it honestly"). So gemini is "**position-dominant, full stop**."
- **The conflict cells say it directly.** In the EARLY (conflict) cells — most-recent-round line early, an older line last — "**both models pick the physically-last twin ~0.69–0.75 of the time, i.e. they pick the chronologically-*earlier* twin against the stamp.** That is text-position following" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"The two headline statistics"). The cell table: claude EARLY ρ_pos 0.688 vs ρ_chron 0.312; gemini EARLY ρ_pos 0.750 vs ρ_chron 0.250.

That is the whole load-bearing fact. The models, when the two cues conflict, are governed by where the text sits, not by the stamped recency. Two bounds travel with it and the essay does not exceed them: the panel is claude + gemini only (gpt dropped because "two harvest + certification attempts showed it cannot supply solo-decodable near-twin descriptions at this difficulty"), and gemini's ≥36 null floor was unmet (min cell 32). The result is `anchor: internal-contrast-only` — "**No human-comparison claim**" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"Caveats").

## What it refines in aggregation-not-constitution

The prior essay does not fall; it is sharpened. Its central deflationary move — that observed convention-*use* is the floor, not the finding, and "anyone who wants to claim interacting LLMs constitute meaning between them now owes [a non-commutative] surplus, not another convergence demo" ([`essay/aggregation-not-constitution`](aggregation-not-constitution.md), §"What the null deflates") — survives intact and gains force. The constitution reading is still not seen: neither model tracks the stamped chronology, and "if anything, in the conflict cells they move *against* it" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"What it means for the conjecture"). No path-dependent, constituted-between convention appeared.

What is refined is the *other* horn of the dichotomy — the AGGREGATION reading itself. The prior essay inherited from [`conjecture/commutative-convention`](../conjectures/commutative-convention.md) a specific picture of aggregation: a convention "computed inside each agent" from the shared content and aggregated, such that "scrambling the order of that content leaves the result unchanged — the aggregated convention is **commutative**" ([`conjecture/commutative-convention`](../conjectures/commutative-convention.md), §"aggregation vs. constitution"). That is **clean set-aggregation**: order-free, content-only, a true bag of turns. The v4 evidence does not support it. The models are "**not** content-only — they are strongly **position-driven** (Δ_pos clean in both). So the null is **not** certified either; v4 does not strengthen the deflationary bet" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"What it means for the conjecture"). A clean bag of turns would be position-insensitive; these models are position-*dominated*.

So the refinement is precise. Aggregation survives as the live family of readings — there is no chronology-tracking surplus to climb the second ladder of [`concept/relational-meaning`](../../base/concepts/relational-meaning.md). But the *form* of aggregation the deflationary picture imagined — the clean, order-free, content-only set — is itself an **idealization the evidence does not bear out**. What the pilot actually shows is **layout-weighted (position-weighted) aggregation**: interpretation is recovered from the record, but weighted by where on the page the lines sit, with the last line dominant. The deflationary "bag of turns" was too clean. The honest replacement is a *bag with a gradient down the page*.

## What a conversation is, to a model

If the clean bag of turns is wrong and the timeline is wrong, what is left? The positive picture the v4 evidence supports — bounded hard by the next section — is that to these two models, in this probe, a conversation is **prose geometry**: a text object with spatial and serial structure, in which the dominant signal is what sits *last on the page*. Not a sequence of timestamped events whose order is part of what the convention *is* (that would be constitution; not seen). Not an order-free set of contents from which order has been abstracted away (that would be clean aggregation; not supported either). A layout.

The shape of the dominance is itself informative and connects the relational finding back to the project's distributional spine. The verifier's reconstruction of gemini shows the position effect is not flat: it is "following position *more strongly in late cells than early cells* (a **serial-position/recency-of-reading gradient**)" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"Reading it honestly"). A serial-position gradient over a text — last-read weighted highest — is exactly the kind of signature a next-token predictor conditioned on a linear context produces *for free*. It is a `distributional`/positional artifact in the sense [`concept/distributional-meaning`](../../base/concepts/distributional-meaning.md) makes precise: structure that the training objective and the linear prompt geometry supply without anything semantic about *recency-as-recency* being read off the stamp. The "conversation" the model reads is, to this probe, the same object any text is: a string with a strong end-weighting, not an interaction with a history.

This sits cleanly inside the deflationary map. [`concept/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning`](../../base/concepts/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning.md) records the relational pilot as a finding that "push[es] toward deflation / the null" — but the deflationary reading it names there is the *clean* one ("two next-token predictors converging from overlapping co-occurrence content … survives order-scrambling"). The refinement this essay adds to that page's spirit (without editing it) is that the deflationary picture is not even as clean as the deflationist assumed: the model is not a serene order-free aggregator but a position-anchored reader of a page. The deflation is, if anything, *more* deflationary — less like averaging shared content, more like reading the bottom line.

## What this does NOT show

This is the essay's discipline, and the bounds are not hedges — they are the result's own ratified limits.

- **No human comparison, in either direction.** The result is `anchor: internal-contrast-only` — "**No human-comparison claim**" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"Caveats"). Nothing here says models differ from, or resemble, humans on position-following, recency, or layout-reading. Humans are simply not in this measurement. The prior essay's anchored human leg ([`source/brennan-clark-1996-conceptual-pacts`](../../base/sources/brennan-clark-1996-conceptual-pacts.md), via [`essay/aggregation-not-constitution`](aggregation-not-constitution.md)) grounds partner-specificity and historicity only, and on the one adjacent measure reports *frequency over recency* — so there is no in-repo human resource that anchors order- or position-sensitivity at all. Any reader who hears "humans use timelines, models use layouts" is importing a contrast the evidence does not carry.
- **The stamp-blindness limit: the models do not *choose* to ignore recency.** This is the sharpest constraint. The result states it as a "Crucial scope limit": "position-following here is **indistinguishable from stamp-blindness** … it does **not** prove the model reads the stamp *values* as recency. So this result must **not** be read as 'the models chose to ignore recency'" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"Reading it honestly"). The design cannot establish whether the models parse the round stamps as recency at all. The defensible statement is only: *when stamped chronology and physical position conflict, the picks are governed by physical position; whether the round numbers are read as recency is not established.* This essay's "a conversation is a layout, not a timeline" must be heard inside that bound — it is a claim about what governs the pick under conflict, not a claim that the model considered and rejected the timeline.
- **The conjecture stays open: neither falsified nor certified.** [`conjecture/commutative-convention`](../conjectures/commutative-convention.md) remains `proposed`. "**Not falsified**" (no chronology-tracking, so no constituted convention) and "**Not certified**" (the models are position-driven, not content-only, so the commutative null is not strengthened) ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"What it means for the conjecture"). This essay does not certify the conjecture and does not retire it.
- **The metaphysics is untouched.** As in the prior essay, a bounded behavioral pilot cannot deflate any *metaphysical* thesis about what meaning-constitution is. What moves here is strictly the **empirical picture of how these two models read a record** — not any claim about whether meaning is constitutively social, or relational, or grounded. The deflationary *metaphysical* theses sit exactly where [`concept/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning`](../../base/concepts/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning.md) leaves them: behavioral evidence "cannot, in principle, close" the existence question. This essay reports a layout-reader; it does not litigate what that reader *is*.
- **Pilot scale.** Two models, gpt dropped, gemini's null floor unmet, forced-label elicitation (which "could change the pick mechanism, so [the verdicts] do not automatically transfer to free-form settings"), and harvested-and-certified — not live-game — stimuli ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"Caveats"). Every sentence above is pilot-scaled.

## The methodological sting (the generalization of the prior essay's lesson)

The prior essay's deepest point was methodological: convergence is the floor, and a claimant "owes that surplus, not another convergence demo" ([`essay/aggregation-not-constitution`](aggregation-not-constitution.md), §"What the null deflates"; the Revision log records its pre-run critic catching a bare prompt-position artifact that would have masqueraded as the non-commutative surplus). The v4 result generalizes that lesson into a named, located confound. A **position-anchored reader can masquerade as either horn of the dichotomy**: whenever the physically-last line coincides with the chronologically-latest turn (the LATE cells, where "ρ_chron ≡ ρ_pos because the latest-stamp line *is* the last line"), a pure position-follower looks exactly like a chronology-tracker — i.e. looks like *constitution*; and a coarse read that never separates position from content can mistake the same reader for clean *aggregation*. This is precisely why three prior versions could not tell the readings apart: v3's verifier "explicitly **refused** to assert 'physical position, not chronology'" because the collinear forward arm could not earn it; "**v4's within-arm decoupling earns exactly that reading cleanly**" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"What it means for the conjecture"). The sting, stated for the whole relational line: "any future chronology/recency probe over a *linear* prompt must decouple stamped recency from physical position, or it cannot distinguish 'tracks the latest convention' from 'reads the last line'" (§"The methodological achievement"). The prior essay said *claimants owe the surplus under artifact-resistant controls*; v4 shows what one such artifact concretely is and how to design it out.

## Revision triggers (read before citing)

This essay is built on a 2-model internal-contrast-only pilot and is explicitly revisable. Stated in advance:

- **(a) A v5 that neutralizes text-position and recovers chronology-tracking.** If a design that removes the position confound (so the last line is not a cue) shows interpretation tracking the stamped chronology with a CI-clean effect, the "layout, not timeline" thesis is **overturned for that setting**: a chronology-tracking surplus would be exactly the non-commutative constitution signal the prior essay said a claimant owes, and this essay's positive picture (prose geometry) would be relativized to the linear-prompt geometry that produced it. The conjecture would then be in play for falsification. The essay moves to `revised` with an in-page log entry.
- **(b) A stamp-comprehension pre-probe.** Because position-following is currently "indistinguishable from stamp-blindness," a pre-probe that establishes the models *do* read the round stamps as recency (in a non-conflict setting) would sharpen the thesis: position-dominance *despite* demonstrated stamp-comprehension would be a stronger result (closer to "reads the page over the timeline even when it can read the timeline"). If instead the models fail such a pre-probe, the thesis weakens to "stamp-blindness," and the "layout, not timeline" framing must be retired in favor of "the recency signal was simply not registered."
- **(c) Cross-family, image, or live-reassignment extensions.** The conjecture's named scope extensions — "**Image referents, cross-family dyads, and live (non-constructed) reassignment**" ([`result/relational-history-perturbation-v4`](../results/relational-history-perturbation-v4.md), §"Caveats") — could each show position-following breaking (a between-agent or non-linear-layout surplus appearing where homogeneous linear-text dyads stay position-dominant). Any such result would **bound** this essay's thesis to the linear-text-grid setting rather than overturn it: "a conversation is a layout" would become "a conversation *presented as a linear text grid* is read as a layout."
- **(d) Free-form elicitation.** The verdicts hold under forced single-label format; a free-form replication could change the pick mechanism. A free-form run that recovered chronology-sensitivity would localize the layout effect to the forced-format probe.

## What this essay is not

Not a claim that models cannot track conversational order — only that, in this probe, under conflict, they did not, and were governed by physical position instead. Not a claim about humans — the human side of every relational contrast in the repo is anchored only on partner-specificity/historicity, never order-sensitivity ([`essay/aggregation-not-constitution`](aggregation-not-constitution.md), §"What this essay is not"). Not a certification or retirement of [`conjecture/commutative-convention`](../conjectures/commutative-convention.md), which stays `proposed`. And not a metaphysical verdict — it reports a layout-reading behavior and refuses to litigate what, if anything, it amounts to.

## Honesty box

- The essay's **original** contribution is the argumentative shape — naming the third reading (layout, not timeline; aggregation that is position-weighted rather than clean) that the v4 decoupling reveals and the prior dichotomy could not see — not any new empirical claim. Every empirical sentence cites the in-repo page that carries it.
- The strongest sentence the evidence supports is bounded three ways at once: *when stamped chronology and physical text-position conflict, in this 2-model internal-contrast-only pilot, both models' picks are governed by physical position* — with "governed by position" **indistinguishable from "blind to the stamp,"** with **no human comparison**, and with the **conjecture neither falsified nor certified**. Nothing here outruns that, and the layout thesis is the interpretation of that bounded fact, offered as a refinement of the prior essay and held revisable by the triggers above.
