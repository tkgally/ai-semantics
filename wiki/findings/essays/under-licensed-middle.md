# The under-licensed middle is a property of the instrument

> **Status: draft (2026-07-03, s174). A philosophical-track essay in the project's own voice.**
> It introduces **no new empirical claim** and makes **no human comparison of its own**. Every
> empirical assertion cites the in-repo `result` or `essay` page that carries it, at that page's
> stated strength. Its original contribution is a **structural re-description of the instrument** —
> that the "under-licensed middle" is the *generic* output of a word-varying matched control, not an
> accident of one probe — plus a methodological caution and one new falsifiable bet. It is **not**
> evidence, and it re-measures nothing. Read the Honesty box before citing. It clears the new-essay
> bar (`PROTOCOL.md §3`) on two grounds: a **fired revision trigger** (the s173 doppelgänger control
> partially discharged the parent essay's first trigger) and a **new falsifiable bet** (the
> cleft-only construction-grain battery, registered below).

## The claim

The parent essay [`essay/shadow-depth-cross-cuts-grain`](shadow-depth-cross-cuts-grain.md) reads the
lexicon–grammar continuum's single test — *does the model track a meaning gradient that beats the
distributional shadow?* — as sorting phenomena along one axis with, in effect, **two poles**: a
phenomenon is either a **shadow-beater** (a measured residual over a shadow-stripping control) or
**shadow-saturated** (a reading on which a surface-cue account has no residual to answer for). When
the presupposition corner's owed matched control ran
([`result/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1`](../results/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1.md)),
it returned neither pole. It returned a **measured third state** the parent essay names but does not
develop: **BEATS-DOPPELGANGER but under-licensed** — the trigger *is* discriminated from a matched
non-presupposing doppelgänger, yet the residual is keyed to the trigger word-form and reconstructable
by a distributional surface-cue learner. The parent calls this "the under-licensed middle" and treats
it as a *placement* on the shadow-depth axis (a reading with a measured caveat).

This essay adds a layer the parent does not contain. It is a claim about the **instrument**, not the
placement:

**The under-licensed middle is not an accident of one probe. It is the generic outcome of any
*word-varying* matched control — and "beater" and "under-licensed middle" are therefore outputs of two
*different* instruments, not two points on one axis.** Instrument grain gates which verdicts are even
reachable. A control that varies the trigger **word** can reach only {saturated, under-licensed}; it
**cannot, by construction, return a clean shadow-beater**. Only a control that holds the words fixed
and varies the **construction** can reach a beater at all. Conflating a word-varying
"beats-doppelgänger" with a construction-varying beater mis-reads the table.

## What s173 measured — the third state, recapped at its stated strength

The doppelgänger control's pre-registered verdict is **BEATS-DOPPELGANGER** (pooled residual
**+0.78 / +0.47 / +0.94**, robust across all nine sensitivity cutoffs)
([`result/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1`](../results/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1.md)).
The clean flat null — the "shadow-saturated" prize the design named — did **not** obtain: the
doppelgänger does not endorse the content equally. But the result page is emphatic that BEATS is **the
under-licensed outcome**. Its own reading: "the panel's endorsement is keyed to the trigger word-form
above bare complement presence," and "A **verb-sensitive surface-cue follower** — which any
distributional next-token learner is — reconstructs this residual, because the powered D1 doppelgängers
vary the **trigger word** (`realize`→`suspect`, `stopped`→`considered`), whose distributions differ."
So the corner "lands in the **under-licensed middle**, not at either clean pole": not the flat null,
not a shadow-beater. That is the fact this essay re-describes.

## A word-varying control cannot return a clean beater

The re-description is a short argument that runs entirely on the result page's own logic, generalized
one step.

A matched *doppelgänger* control isolates a construct by pairing each trigger item with a minimally
different control item and reading the behavioral gap. There are two ways to build the pair: swap the
trigger **word** (leaving the frame), or swap the **construction** (leaving the words). The
factive/aspectual doppelgängers take the first route — `realize`→`suspect`, `stopped`→`considered` —
and this fixes what the residual can mean. Two different words *have different distributions*; a
[`distributional`](../../base/concepts/distributional-meaning.md) next-token learner that follows the
surface cue at verb-level resolution will, for that reason alone, endorse the trigger's complement more
than the doppelgänger's. So a positive residual from a word-swap is **guaranteed reconstructable** by a
surface-cue account — it is what such an account *predicts*, not a residual over and above it.

That guarantee collapses the reachable outcomes of a word-varying control to exactly two:

- a **flat null** — the doppelgänger is endorsed equally (saturated: the crudest bare-complement
  follower); or
- an **under-licensed middle** — the trigger is discriminated, but the discrimination is a word-form
  cue the distributional learner reconstructs.

A **clean shadow-beater — a residual a surface-cue account cannot reconstruct — is not in the set.** It
is excluded not by how the models behaved but by how the instrument was built. This is the essay's
original move: the under-licensed middle is the *generic* verdict of a word-varying doppelgänger, the
place it lands whenever it lands anywhere above flat.

## Two instruments, not two points on one axis

A clean beater requires the *other* instrument: hold the words fixed and vary only the construction.
That is exactly the comparative-correlative design. All three models assert a covariation direction
for "~100% of CC items but for only 10–20% of the matched non-CC controls that **reuse the same
words**" — an assertion gap of **+80–90 pp** that is "direct evidence that the **construction**, not
the scalar vocabulary, drives the inference"
([`result/comparative-correlative-covariation-v1`](../results/comparative-correlative-covariation-v1.md)).
Because the control shares the trigger's words, a word-sensitive surface-cue follower has no
word-distribution difference to exploit; the residual survives *that* shadow. The result page draws
the disanalogy itself: "the CC clears controls that reuse the **same words** and vary only the
construction; these D1 controls vary the word."

So the two verdicts sit on two instruments with different reachable sets:

| matched control | what it varies | reachable verdicts | can it license a beater? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **lexical-grain doppelgänger** | the trigger **word** (frame fixed) | {saturated, under-licensed} | **No — excluded by construction** |
| **construction-grain doppelgänger** | the **construction** (words fixed) | {saturated, under-licensed, beater} | **Yes — necessary, not sufficient** |

The decisive content is the last column: a word-swap **excludes** `beater`; a construction-swap
**admits** it — and the comparative correlative is the existence proof that it can be reached. But
construction-grain is *necessary, not sufficient*. Holding the words fixed removes the *word*-cue route
to under-licensing; it does not remove a *frame*-cue route. The one construction-grain leg inside the
presupposition probe — the cleft, "the *only* powered leg that holds the content words constant and
varies only the construction" — shows this cautionary case directly. On the one model where a clean
construction-grain control is available, "gpt-5.4-mini control-FAILS the cleft (trigger_project 0.58 <
0.60)" and its cleft residual is "+0.08 — essentially FLAT"; where a cleft residual does appear
(claude, gemini) it "is thus itself a negation-frame surface-cue difference"
([`result/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1`](../results/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1.md)).
So a construction-grain control can still land saturated (gpt) or in the under-licensed middle via a
*frame* cue (claude/gemini) — it merely *can*, unlike a word-swap, also reach a genuine beater. The
asymmetry is about what is *reachable*, not what is *guaranteed*.

This is why "beater" and "under-licensed middle" are not two readings of one measurement. They are the
characteristic outputs of two instruments of different grain, and the grain gates the verdict before
the model is ever run.

## The corner's non-beater status is partly the instrument, not only the phenomenon

Read through this lens, the presupposition corner's non-beater result is partly a property of how it
was probed. Two of the three powered families that carried the pooled verdict — factive and aspectual
— are **word-varying**. Their strong residuals (factive +0.83 / +0.58 / +0.83; aspectual +1.00 / +0.75
/ +1.00) are exactly the residuals a word-swap **cannot** convert into a beater, however large they
are; they were structurally confined to {saturated, under-licensed} before the run. The one family
that *could* have delivered a construction-grain beater — the cleft — gave "no clean cross-panel
residual" (BEATS 2/3, gpt control-failing, the claude/gemini signal a negation-cell surface cue)
([`result/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1`](../results/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1.md)).

So the corner did not "fail to beat the shadow" in the way the comparative correlative *passed*. On the
legs that dominated the verdict, beating the shadow was **not an available outcome**; on the one leg
where it was available, it did not occur cleanly. That is a genuinely new, somewhat deflationary
sharpening of the parent essay's "measured caveat": the presupposition corner sits below the beater
boundary of the flagship table ([`theory/shadow-depth-table-v1`](../theory/shadow-depth-table-v1.md))
**partly because the instrument that dominated its measurement could not, by construction, place it
above** — not only because the phenomenon is deep-shadow. The two contributions are entangled in the
current probe, and honesty requires reporting the entanglement rather than reading the whole result off
onto the phenomenon. The table's placement stands; what changes is the *reason* the reader should
attach to it.

## Generality: the caution binds the whole program

The point is not special to presupposition. It is a property of the **doppelgänger instrument**
whenever the doppelgänger is built by swapping the trigger word, and it therefore binds every future
probe in the program that reaches for such a control:

**A "beats-doppelgänger" verdict from any word-varying matched control is under-licensed by
construction. It must be reported as a word-form-keyed discrimination — a lexical/constructional
contrast that is itself distributionally encoded — never as beating the distributional shadow.**

The lexical pole's saturated corner illustrates the same discipline from the other side. Antonymy is
placed at the deep-shadow end by [`essay/antonymy-outlier-distributional-shadow`](antonymy-outlier-distributional-shadow.md),
and the discriminating control that essay names as owed is a **contrastive-frame distributional
baseline** — a control that supplies the antonym-cuing frame statistics — **not** a word-swap. That is
the correct instrument for a saturated-corner test: it holds the shadow-bearing material in view and
asks whether anything clears daylight above it. The presupposition line should learn the same lesson.
Its word-varying legs were never going to license a beater; only a control that fixes the shadow-cues
and varies the structure can, and even then only if it also closes the frame-cue route.

Both [`inferential`](../../base/concepts/inferential-meaning.md) competences at issue here —
presupposition projection (a represented presupposition/assertion split that ought to hold invariantly)
and comparative-correlative covariation — are the kind of thing a beater instrument is *for*: an
inference-licensing residual over and above co-occurrence. The instrument caution says only that you
cannot detect such a residual with a probe whose positive verdict co-occurrence already predicts.

## Revision triggers (read before citing)

- **The cleft-only, construction-grain doppelgänger battery is the one design that could move the
  corner out of the under-licensed middle — new falsifiable bet.** The result page names it: "a
  **construction-grain-only** doppelgänger battery (cleft-family expansion, negation-only
  discriminating cell, controlling the question-frame confound) is the one leg that could in principle
  reach a residual not reducible to a lexical distribution — still bounded by B1"
  ([`result/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1`](../results/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1.md)).
  This essay registers the bet on that instrument. **If** such a battery yields a residual not
  reducible to a negation-frame surface cue, held across the discriminating cells, the presupposition
  corner moves toward the beater side and this refinement is discharged in the *confirming* direction.
  **If** it too lands flat or in the under-licensed middle (the residual reducible to a frame cue), the
  corner is **settled as a non-beater on the strongest available instrument**, and the "partly the
  instrument" caveat above converts to "measured non-beater, construction-grain." Either outcome
  discharges the bet; a word-varying re-run does **not** (it cannot reach the beater verdict and so
  cannot settle the question).
- **If the parent essay's `shadow-saturated` placement of the presupposition corner retracts** (e.g.
  its environment-gated reading is overturned, or a beater is measured), this refinement moves with it:
  the instrument-structure argument is a *reading of why* the corner sits where the parent places it,
  so if the placement moves, the reading is re-examined in-page.
- **If the underlying numbers move** — the pooled residuals, the CC assertion gap, or the cleft
  control-fail — the empirical premises above move with them and this reading is re-examined.

## Honesty box

- The **original** contribution is a **re-description of the instrument**, not evidence: that a
  word-varying matched control's reachable verdicts are exactly {saturated, under-licensed}, so the
  under-licensed middle is the *generic* output of that instrument and a clean beater is only reachable
  by a construction-grain control that also closes the frame-cue route. The names ("shadow-beater",
  "shadow-saturated", "under-licensed middle") and the placements are the parent essay's
  ([`essay/shadow-depth-cross-cuts-grain`](shadow-depth-cross-cuts-grain.md)); this essay argues they
  are outputs of **two instruments** and draws the methodological caution and the bet. It re-measures
  nothing.
- **No empirical claim here is new, original, or reported, and there is no human comparison.** Every
  number is cited at its source page's stated strength: the pooled residuals **+0.78 / +0.47 / +0.94**,
  the per-family cells (factive +0.83 / +0.58 / +0.83; aspectual +1.00 / +0.75 / +1.00; cleft BEATS
  2/3, gpt control-fail trigger_project 0.58 < 0.60, residual +0.08), the word-swap reconstruction
  logic, and the "under-licensed middle" placement all from
  [`result/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1`](../results/presupposition-doppelganger-control-v1.md);
  the CC assertion gap **+80–90 pp** and the "same words" control from
  [`result/comparative-correlative-covariation-v1`](../results/comparative-correlative-covariation-v1.md).
  The presupposition inputs are within-model contrasts with no human baseline; this essay adds none.
- **Weak evidential strength, offered as the reading to knock down.** The whole structure rests on
  n=3 models, small synthetic item sets, single runs, and direction-of-effect only. The instrument
  argument is a logical re-description of *those* probes; it is not a proof that every future
  word-varying control on every phenomenon must behave this way, only that a positive verdict from one
  is under-licensed by the same construction. The strongest thing the essay asserts is that
  **instrument grain gates which shadow-depth verdicts are reachable**, and that a "beats-doppelgänger"
  from a word-swap must never be reported as beating the distributional shadow — offered as a
  methodological caution to falsify, not as a demonstrated property of language models.
