Explicit cheat-surface check

No residual cheat-surface in the shadow control's logic (B1–B3 sound and sufficient). Two residual cheat-surfaces at the build/authoring level + one power concern (all freeze-time):

  1. Orthographic animacy-telegraphing in the nonce arm. A nonce possessor's animacy must be conveyed by the frame/gloss, not by the nonce string's own form (agentive -er, name-like phonotactics, capitalization) — else a pure form-cue reader ("animate-looking strings take 's") scores CONFIRM without semantic animacy. → R1.
  2. The covariate leg of B3 is near-vacuous if the covariate is underpowered. A noisy covariate absorbs little collinear variance, so "animacy survives with the covariate included" becomes trivially true, meaning CONFIRM effectively rests on the nonce arm — acceptable only if the nonce arm is genuinely clean (R1) and the covariate's own explanatory power is reported. → R2.
