- Q2-B is weakly motivated as a new exploratory hypothesis, but it is not an H2 replication and should be labeled exploratory/new theory, not ratified as part of this probe.
- Bottom line: ratify Q1-C and Q2-A, but keep the across-relation H1 claim explicitly low-power/secondary; don’t overstate “replication” beyond the adjective antonymy-specific question.
