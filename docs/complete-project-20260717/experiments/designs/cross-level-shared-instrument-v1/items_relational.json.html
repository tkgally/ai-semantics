note
FRESH FROZEN synthetic relational records, authored for this build. internal-contrast-only: author-constructed; NO human-annotation claim (Q4). Record format follows experiments/designs/relational-spontaneous-recency-v5-optionA.md: a small grid of distinct figures, a stamped history binding a coined term, and a query asking which figure the term refers to at the queried moment. Each ambiguous-midrecord item has the term bound to TWO figures with NO recency/frequency signal to break the tie (the two bindings carry the SAME round stamp, and the term is NOT reassigned-with-recency) -- so the binding is genuinely underdetermined at the queried moment. Clear-class controls bind the term to ONE figure unambiguously. The relational 'aggregate' is DISCLOSED as the weaker within-record / single-reader-recoverable notion (gate Q3).
