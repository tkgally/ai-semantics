verdict_states (4 fields)
channel_controlled
gpt now WORKS the steps (uptake forced) AND its commitment posture is unchanged -- bridging confidence not CI-strict lower than both clear classes AND bridging decline not elevated. gpt's ungraded-commitment null is then channel-controlled (joins gemini), and the lexical leg's 3/3 channel check is complete.
graded_under_surface
gpt works the steps AND its commitment posture CRACKS on BOTH B and C (bridging confidence CI-strict lower AND decline elevated). Reported per the frozen rule as graded commitment under a forced surface for gpt.
mixed_weak
gpt works the steps but B and C DISAGREE (e.g. confidence softens but decline does not, the claude-offered pattern) -> MIXED/WEAK per modification 3, NOT the null and NOT a clean positive. C4 of the resolved cross-level gate applies in spirit: a confidence shift unaccompanied by a decline shift is a self-report effect, not graded-moment evidence.
still_declined
gpt STILL emits bare answers despite the forcing (uptake not achieved) -> inconclusive (channel-not-taken-up), reported as such, never as a survival.
