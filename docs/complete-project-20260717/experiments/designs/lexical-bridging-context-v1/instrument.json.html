reading_rule_frozen (8 fields)
_basisoperationalization gate modifications 2 & 3; per-axis, NOT blanket AND.
position_axis
certified by B (b_rel) ALONE: bridging class mean relatedness sits STRICTLY between clear-different mean and clear-same mean (per-lemma-clustered bootstrap CI). Absolute 'intermediate' descriptor: bridging mean within the frozen [40,60] band.
confidence_dispersion_axis
certified by BOTH B and C: (B) bridging mean confidence (b_conf) LOWER than BOTH clear classes; AND (C) bridging decline rate (c_third %UNCLEAR) ELEVATED vs BOTH clear classes.
prediction4_supportediff position holds (B) AND confidence/dispersion holds on BOTH B and C.
mixed_weak_not_null
B-C disagreement on the confidence/dispersion axis is reported as a MIXED/WEAK result with both readings shown — NEVER collapsed into the clean null.
clean_null
bridging handled at clear-item confidence (B) AND clear-item decline rate (C) — a graded SCALE with ungraded COMMITMENT (within-item discreteness). Reported AS CLEANLY as a positive.
clear_class_precondition
interpretable only if the clear classes show high confidence (B: high mean b_conf and saturated b_rel at the poles) AND low decline rate (C: low %UNCLEAR). Report as a precondition; if unmet even with the WiC pole supplement, NO-GO → collapse to internal-contrast-only (anchor conditions 2 & 6).
no_post_hoc_threshold
no pass bar tuned after the run; all contrasts are ordinal with per-lemma-clustered bootstrap uncertainty. A is characterizing-only and never enters this rule.
