Essay: a conversation is a text, not a timeline

Status: revised (2026-06-16). Both stated revision triggers have fired; the core thesis is SCOPED, not deleted. When this essay was drafted it rested on one within-arm decoupling result (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4) and stated, in advance, two revision triggers: (a) a v5 that neutralizes text-position and recovers chronology-tracking, and (b) a stamp-comprehension pre-probe. Both have now run. Option B (result/relational-stamp-comprehension-b) found both models comprehend the round stamp on demand (PASS). Option A (result/relational-spontaneous-recency-a) neutralized physical position and found that when stamped recency is the sole disambiguator, both models track the timeline — spontaneously, at ceiling (SPONT latest-binding rate 1.000, physical-position at chance), now promoted to claim/relational-order-sensitive-reassignment. The "layout, not timeline" thesis is therefore not true simpliciter — it holds only in v4's regime, where chronology was confounded with physical position. The corrected picture is regime-dependent: the models read both cues and which governs depends on the task. The sentences below that frame the model as "a layout, full stop" / "prose geometry" / "a bag with a gradient down the page" are kept visible but marked as scoped to the v4 (position-available) regime; read the Revision log (newest-first, near the end) and the updated "What this does NOT show" section before citing any sentence here. The result remains anchor: internal-contrast-only — no human-comparison claim in either direction. (Strengthened 2026-06-17, not changed: the implicit-reassignment control (result/relational-implicit-reassignment-control) dropped Option A's explicit "was reassigned" sentence and the latest-binding-wins behaviour persisted at ceiling in both models — so the Option-A recency-tracking on which this essay's scoped picture leans is not a wording artifact. The scoped, regime-dependent conclusion is unchanged.) (Also folded in 2026-06-17: a rung-(ii) integration result (result/relational-integration-rung-ii) adds a third regime — when two turns are compatible, both models compose them and the answer is the conjunction, with physical-position-following again at chance (0.250) — reinforcing the regime-dependent reading below; see the Revision log. The scoped conclusion is unchanged.)

The position

The prior essay — essay/aggregation-not-constitution — framed a clean two-way choice for how a model reads a conversational record. Either interpretation tracks the live path/order of the exchange (CONSTITUTION; the convention is non-commutative, in the algebra of conjecture/commutative-convention), or it is recovered from the order-free content set — the "bag of turns" (AGGREGATION; commutative). The pilot null behind that essay found aggregation, and the essay's discipline was to claim no more than that the premature constitution reading falls.

This essay argues that the within-arm decoupling result breaks the dichotomy itself, and names the third thing the break reveals. When stamped chronology and physical text-position are decoupled inside a single presentation arm — chronology carried by an explicit per-line round stamp, the chronologically-latest line deliberately placed non-terminally — both models in the pilot anchor on physical text position, not on stated chronology. In the conflict cells they pick the physically-last line's twin (which is the chronologically-earlier twin) roughly 0.69–0.75 of the time (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"The two headline statistics"). So the model is neither the constitution reader (it does not track the stated chronology) nor the clean commutative bag-of-turns (it is strongly position-driven, not content-only — both position CIs are clean).

The thesis, as drafted, was this: to these models, in this probe, a conversation is a text object with spatial/serial structure — a layout — not a timeline and not an order-free content set. The reading the v4 evidence supports is prose geometry, dominated by what sits last on the page. Everything below is bounded by the result's own honesty constraints, and the bound is severe: position-following here is indistinguishable from stamp-blindness, the conjecture stays open, and no human comparison is licensed. The thesis names a located confound and a positive picture of the model-internal kind; it claims nothing about humans and nothing metaphysical.

SCOPED (2026-06-16) — the thesis above holds only in v4's regime, not simpliciter. Two later runs corrected the unqualified "layout, not timeline" reading. The two caveats stated above as "severe" are both now relaxed: the stamp-blindness bound was resolved by Option B (the models are not stamp-blind — they read the stamp on demand, both PASS, result/relational-stamp-comprehension-b), and the conjecture no longer "stays open" — it is retired/falsified (conjecture/commutative-convention, status RETIRED). Above all, Option A neutralized physical position and made stamped recency the sole disambiguator, and found both models do track the timeline: "SPONT latest-binding rate = 1.000 for both … Physical-position-following is at exactly chance" (result/relational-spontaneous-recency-a, §"The headline"). So the corrected picture is regime-dependent, not "layout simpliciter": the models lean on the salient surface cue (text position) when it is available and sufficient, but recover and use stamped recency when it is the only thing that disambiguates. Read every "layout, not timeline" sentence below as scoped to the v4 (position-available) regime; the Revision log states the full correction. The no-human-comparison and no-metaphysics bounds are untouched and still hold.

The evidence, at its actual strength

The decisive design move is in the result page: chronology is carried by an explicit stamp ("- Round k: …"), the most-recent-round line is placed either physically last or non-terminally, and "crossing DPOS with which twin owns the latest stamp makes chronologically-latest twin and physically-last-line twin balanced and orthogonal within one arm (cov 0; asserted at build, re-derived by the verifier with 0 mismatches)" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"What v4 changed"). Two statistics, both centered on 0.5: Δ_chron (picks the chronologically-latest twin) and Δ_pos (picks the physically-last-line twin). A pure chronology reader gives Δ_pos ≈ 0; a pure position reader gives Δ_chron ≈ 0.

The headline numbers, verbatim from the result page:

  • claude → TEXT-POSITION ARTIFACT. Δ_pos is CI-clean at 0.698 [0.594, 0.804]; Δ_chron is null at 0.509 [0.465, 0.556] (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"The two headline statistics"). Per the frozen decision rule this is "a methodological finding about prompt geometry; it says nothing relational" (§"Reading it honestly").
  • gemini → INCONCLUSIVE/MIXED, same direction. Position-dominant: Δ_pos 0.812 [0.695, 0.930] (clean); ρ_chron 0.562 [0.516, 0.609] marginally off 0.5. The post-run verifier showed that elevation "carries zero residual chronology signal" — a pure-position reader with gemini's own late/early position rates is "forced to produce ρ_chron = (0.875 + (1 − 0.750)) / 2 = 0.5625 — identical to the observed value to the digit" (§"Reading it honestly"). So gemini is "position-dominant, full stop."
  • The conflict cells say it directly. In the EARLY (conflict) cells — most-recent-round line early, an older line last — "both models pick the physically-last twin ~0.69–0.75 of the time, i.e. they pick the chronologically-earlier twin against the stamp. That is text-position following" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"The two headline statistics"). The cell table: claude EARLY ρ_pos 0.688 vs ρ_chron 0.312; gemini EARLY ρ_pos 0.750 vs ρ_chron 0.250.

That is the whole load-bearing fact. The models, when the two cues conflict, are governed by where the text sits, not by the stamped recency. Two bounds travel with it and the essay does not exceed them: the panel is claude + gemini only (gpt dropped because "two harvest + certification attempts showed it cannot supply solo-decodable near-twin descriptions at this difficulty"), and gemini's ≥36 null floor was unmet (min cell 32). The result is anchor: internal-contrast-only — "No human-comparison claim" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"Caveats").

What it refines in aggregation-not-constitution

The prior essay does not fall; it is sharpened. Its central deflationary move — that observed convention-use is the floor, not the finding, and "anyone who wants to claim interacting LLMs constitute meaning between them now owes [a non-commutative] surplus, not another convergence demo" (essay/aggregation-not-constitution, §"What the null deflates") — survives intact and gains force. The constitution reading is still not seen: neither model tracks the stamped chronology, and "if anything, in the conflict cells they move against it" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"What it means for the conjecture"). No path-dependent, constituted-between convention appeared.

What is refined is the other horn of the dichotomy — the AGGREGATION reading itself. The prior essay inherited from conjecture/commutative-convention a specific picture of aggregation: a convention "computed inside each agent" from the shared content and aggregated, such that "scrambling the order of that content leaves the result unchanged — the aggregated convention is commutative" (conjecture/commutative-convention, §"aggregation vs. constitution"). That is clean set-aggregation: order-free, content-only, a true bag of turns. The v4 evidence does not support it. The models are "not content-only — they are strongly position-driven (Δ_pos clean in both). So the null is not certified either; v4 does not strengthen the deflationary bet" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"What it means for the conjecture"). A clean bag of turns would be position-insensitive; these models are position-dominated.

So the refinement is precise. Aggregation survives as the live family of readings — there is no chronology-tracking surplus to climb the second ladder of concept/relational-meaning. But the form of aggregation the deflationary picture imagined — the clean, order-free, content-only set — is itself an idealization the evidence does not bear out. What the pilot actually shows in this regime is layout-weighted (position-weighted) aggregation: interpretation is recovered from the record, but weighted by where on the page the lines sit, with the last line dominant. The deflationary "bag of turns" was too clean. The honest replacement is a bag with a gradient down the page. (Scoped 2026-06-16: this "bag with a gradient down the page" framing is true of the v4 position-available regime only. Option A shows that when physical position is neutralized and stamped recency is the sole disambiguator, the gradient the models follow is not down-the-page at all — it is the stamped timeline, "SPONT latest-binding rate = 1.000 … Physical-position-following is at exactly chance" (result/relational-spontaneous-recency-a, §"The headline"). So position-weighting is the heuristic when position is available and sufficient, not a fixed property of how these models aggregate.)

What a conversation is, to a model

SCOPED (2026-06-16). The "prose geometry / a layout, full stop" reading in this section is the v4-regime picture and is not true where order disambiguates. In Option A's regime — physical position neutralized, stamped recency the sole disambiguator — the models track the timeline at ceiling (result/relational-spontaneous-recency-a). What follows is kept visible as the original argument for the position-available case; read "a layout" everywhere below as "a layout when the last-on-page cue is available and sufficient."

If the clean bag of turns is wrong and the timeline is wrong, what is left? The positive picture the v4 evidence supports — bounded hard by the next section — is that to these two models, in this probe, a conversation is prose geometry: a text object with spatial and serial structure, in which the dominant signal is what sits last on the page. Not a sequence of timestamped events whose order is part of what the convention is (that would be constitution; not seen). Not an order-free set of contents from which order has been abstracted away (that would be clean aggregation; not supported either). A layout. (Scoped: in the v4 regime only. Where order is the sole disambiguator and physical position is neutralized — Option A — the models read the timeline, so "a layout, full stop" is false there; the corrected, unified reading is "they read both cues, and which governs depends on the task.")

The shape of the dominance is itself informative and connects the relational finding back to the project's distributional spine. The verifier's reconstruction of gemini shows the position effect is not flat: it is "following position more strongly in late cells than early cells (a serial-position/recency-of-reading gradient)" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"Reading it honestly"). A serial-position gradient over a text — last-read weighted highest — is exactly the kind of signature a next-token predictor conditioned on a linear context produces for free. It is a distributional/positional artifact in the sense concept/distributional-meaning makes precise: structure that the training objective and the linear prompt geometry supply without anything semantic about recency-as-recency being read off the stamp. The "conversation" the model reads is, to this probe, the same object any text is: a string with a strong end-weighting, not an interaction with a history.

This sits cleanly inside the deflationary map. concept/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning records the relational pilot as a finding that "push[es] toward deflation / the null" — but the deflationary reading it names there is the clean one ("two next-token predictors converging from overlapping co-occurrence content … survives order-scrambling"). The refinement this essay adds to that page's spirit (without editing it) is that the deflationary picture is not even as clean as the deflationist assumed: the model is not a serene order-free aggregator but a position-anchored reader of a page. The deflation is, if anything, more deflationary — less like averaging shared content, more like reading the bottom line.

What this does NOT show

This is the essay's discipline, and the bounds are not hedges — they are the result's own ratified limits.

  • No human comparison, in either direction. The result is anchor: internal-contrast-only — "No human-comparison claim" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"Caveats"). Nothing here says models differ from, or resemble, humans on position-following, recency, or layout-reading. Humans are simply not in this measurement. The prior essay's anchored human leg (source/brennan-clark-1996-conceptual-pacts, via essay/aggregation-not-constitution) grounds partner-specificity and historicity only, and on the one adjacent measure reports frequency over recency — so there is no in-repo human resource that anchors order- or position-sensitivity at all. Any reader who hears "humans use timelines, models use layouts" is importing a contrast the evidence does not carry.
  • ~~The stamp-blindness limit~~ — RESOLVED (2026-06-16): the models are not stamp-blind. As drafted, this bullet read: "position-following here is indistinguishable from stamp-blindness … the design cannot establish whether the models parse the round stamps as recency at all." That caveat is now settled. The Option B pre-probe directly asked the recency question with physical position neutralized, and both models score 1.000 reading the stamp as a recency value: "Both models comprehend the round stamp as a recency value on demand" (result/relational-stamp-comprehension-b, §banner). So v4's position-following is not stamp-blindness; it is best read as comprehending-but-not-spontaneously-using in v4's task (the models can read the stamp; they just did not weight it when the last-on-page cue was available). Option A then went further — when the stamp was the sole disambiguator, the models did spontaneously weight it. The defensible statement is now: the models comprehend stamped recency on demand, lean on physical position in v4's regime, and spontaneously use stamped recency when it is the sole disambiguator.
  • ~~The conjecture stays open~~ — RETIRED/FALSIFIED (2026-06-16). As drafted, this bullet said the conjecture "remains proposed … neither falsified nor certified," and that the essay "does not retire it." That is now false. conjecture/commutative-convention is RETIRED — FALSIFIED: Option A "constructs the setting this conjecture's own caveat 2 flagged as missing … and finds both models recover the term by its most-recent binding, spontaneously and at ceiling" (conjecture/commutative-convention, §banner). The recovered convention is a function of the recency order, not the content set — i.e. non-commutative — so the falsification clause fired in the chronology direction. Two bounds travel with that, unchanged: it is order-sensitivity, not constitution ("consistent with a thin latest-binding-wins / convention-update rule … Bottom rung, not top," conjecture/commutative-convention, §"Update 2026-06-16 (Option A)"), and the v1/v4 nulls are not overturned but bounded ("commutativity is operationalization-dependent: present where order carries no disambiguating information, absent where it does," ibid.).
  • The metaphysics is untouched. As in the prior essay, a bounded behavioral pilot cannot deflate any metaphysical thesis about what meaning-constitution is. What moves here is strictly the empirical picture of how these two models read a record — not any claim about whether meaning is constitutively social, or relational, or grounded. The deflationary metaphysical theses sit exactly where concept/deflationary-and-eliminativist-llm-meaning leaves them: behavioral evidence "cannot, in principle, close" the existence question. This essay reports a layout-reader; it does not litigate what that reader is.
  • Pilot scale. Two models, gpt dropped, gemini's null floor unmet, forced-label elicitation (which "could change the pick mechanism, so [the verdicts] do not automatically transfer to free-form settings"), and harvested-and-certified — not live-game — stimuli (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"Caveats"). Every sentence above is pilot-scaled.

The methodological sting (the generalization of the prior essay's lesson)

The prior essay's deepest point was methodological: convergence is the floor, and a claimant "owes that surplus, not another convergence demo" (essay/aggregation-not-constitution, §"What the null deflates"; the Revision log records its pre-run critic catching a bare prompt-position artifact that would have masqueraded as the non-commutative surplus). The v4 result generalizes that lesson into a named, located confound. A position-anchored reader can masquerade as either horn of the dichotomy: whenever the physically-last line coincides with the chronologically-latest turn (the LATE cells, where "ρ_chron ≡ ρ_pos because the latest-stamp line is the last line"), a pure position-follower looks exactly like a chronology-tracker — i.e. looks like constitution; and a coarse read that never separates position from content can mistake the same reader for clean aggregation. This is precisely why three prior versions could not tell the readings apart: v3's verifier "explicitly refused to assert 'physical position, not chronology'" because the collinear forward arm could not earn it; "v4's within-arm decoupling earns exactly that reading cleanly" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"What it means for the conjecture"). The sting, stated for the whole relational line: "any future chronology/recency probe over a linear prompt must decouple stamped recency from physical position, or it cannot distinguish 'tracks the latest convention' from 'reads the last line'" (§"The methodological achievement"). The prior essay said claimants owe the surplus under artifact-resistant controls; v4 shows what one such artifact concretely is and how to design it out.

This section is untouched by the 2026-06-16 revision — and arguably strengthened by it. Option A is precisely the decoupled design the sting demanded: physical text-position was neutralized by balanced rotation so that choosing the latest-bound figure could not be the position artifact, and the answer changed. With position decoupled, "Physical-position-following is at exactly chance (pick==last-line 0.250, pick==first-line 0.250) — so this is genuine stamp-recency reading, not the text-position artifact v4 found. The geometry decoupling (Option A's whole point) worked" (result/relational-spontaneous-recency-a, §"The headline"). The sting predicted that an undecoupled linear probe cannot tell "reads the last line" from "tracks the latest convention"; decoupling it flipped the verdict from the former to the latter. That is the methodological lesson doing exactly the work it was stated to do — which is why the located-confound material here stands, even as the "layout simpliciter" reading it once supported is now scoped.

Revision triggers (read before citing)

This essay was built on a 2-model internal-contrast-only pilot and is explicitly revisable. The triggers were stated in advance; (a) and (b) have now both fired (see the Revision log below):

  • (a) A v5 that neutralizes text-position and recovers chronology-tracking. — FIRED (2026-06-16). If a design that removes the position confound (so the last line is not a cue) shows interpretation tracking the stamped chronology with a CI-clean effect, the "layout, not timeline" thesis is overturned for that setting: a chronology-tracking surplus would be exactly the non-commutative constitution signal the prior essay said a claimant owes, and this essay's positive picture (prose geometry) would be relativized to the linear-prompt geometry that produced it. The conjecture would then be in play for falsification. The essay moves to revised with an in-page log entry. — Fired exactly as written: Option A (result/relational-spontaneous-recency-a) neutralized position and recovered chronology-tracking at ceiling (SPONT latest-binding 1.000, position at chance); the conjecture is falsified/retired; the essay is now revised. Note the result is "order-sensitivity, not constitution" (a thin latest-binding-wins rule), so this is the falsification signal, scoped to the bottom rung, not a full constitution surplus.
  • (b) A stamp-comprehension pre-probe. — FIRED (2026-06-16). Because position-following is currently "indistinguishable from stamp-blindness," a pre-probe that establishes the models do read the round stamps as recency (in a non-conflict setting) would sharpen the thesis: position-dominance despite demonstrated stamp-comprehension would be a stronger result (closer to "reads the page over the timeline even when it can read the timeline"). If instead the models fail such a pre-probe, the thesis weakens to "stamp-blindness," and the "layout, not timeline" framing must be retired in favor of "the recency signal was simply not registered." — Fired: Option B (result/relational-stamp-comprehension-b) — both models PASS at 1.000. So the v4 position-following is comprehending-but-not-spontaneously-using, not stamp-blindness; the "recency signal was simply not registered" escape is closed.
  • (c) Cross-family, image, or live-reassignment extensions. The conjecture's named scope extensions — "Image referents, cross-family dyads, and live (non-constructed) reassignment" (result/relational-history-perturbation-v4, §"Caveats") — could each show position-following breaking (a between-agent or non-linear-layout surplus appearing where homogeneous linear-text dyads stay position-dominant). Any such result would bound this essay's thesis to the linear-text-grid setting rather than overturn it: "a conversation is a layout" would become "a conversation presented as a linear text grid is read as a layout."
  • (d) Free-form elicitation. The verdicts hold under forced single-label format; a free-form replication could change the pick mechanism. A free-form run that recovered chronology-sensitivity would localize the layout effect to the forced-format probe.

Revision log (newest first)

2026-06-17 — rung-(ii) integration ADDS a third regime (not a trigger firing; status stays revised)

Like the implicit-reassignment control below, this is a fold-in, not a named trigger (a)–(d): the rung-(ii) probe is a new rung of the relational ladder, not a re-run of the v4/v5 history-perturbation test the triggers turn on.

What it tested. Whether a compatible earlier turn survives (integration) or is overwritten — a coined term constrained over two stamped rounds (an earlier shape, a latest pattern) so only the conjunction is unique (result/relational-integration-rung-ii; independent pre-run critic GO, post-run verifier REPRODUCED).

Result (verbatim). Both models integrate at ceiling: INTEG target rate 1.000 (Wilson [0.926, 1.000]), pure-overwrite "latest only" 0.000, grid-position-following at exactly chance (0.250 / 0.250), DIRECT on-demand 1.000, 0 NA. "the answer is the conjunction, not a single attribute and not a position artifact" (result/relational-integration-rung-ii, §"The headline").

What it adds to this essay's picture. A third regime, and it sharpens the essay's central scoping claim. The v4 regime gave position-weighted layout-reading; Option A's regime gave timeline-tracking when stamped recency is the sole disambiguator; the integration regime now gives composition of compatible turns, with physical position once more at chance. Three regimes, three governing cues — decisive confirmation that "a conversation is a layout, full stop" is false as a general claim and true only of v4's position-available regime: the models read whichever cue the task makes load-bearing (text position in v4; stamped recency in Option A; the conjunction of both turns here). The "bag with a gradient down the page" framing is, exactly as the essay already scopes it, a v4-only heuristic — here the gradient is not down the page at all (position at chance) but onto the both-match figure.

What it does NOT change. The scoped, regime-dependent conclusion stands; status stays revised. Still internal-contrast-only — no human comparison. Still thin, not rich: composing two stated constraints is single-reader-recoverable ("a single reader handed the record can compose the two stated constraints," result/relational-integration-rung-ii, §"What this shows"), so the fuller update rule (supersede-on-conflict, compose-on-compatibility) climbs one rung on the same (thin) side of the cut — no move toward constitution. Two result-borne bounds carry: the design is symmetric (shows survival, not order-sensitive composition) and the conjunction is trivially easy (DIRECT = INTEG = 1.000, so the ceiling is weak evidence of genuinely spontaneous composition).

2026-06-17 — implicit-reassignment control STRENGTHENS the Option-A regime (not a trigger firing; status stays revised)

This is a strengthening, not a trigger firing: the implicit-reassignment control is not one of this essay's named triggers (a)–(d). It is a single-factor control of Option A — the positive on which this essay's scoped, regime-dependent picture leans — and it makes that positive sturdier without changing anything else.

What it tested. Option A's clean positive carried one deflationary escape: maybe the models tracked recency only because the INTRO told them the term "was reassigned … in different rounds you agreed it referred to different figures." The control (result/relational-implicit-reassignment-control) removed exactly that sentence, kept everything else byte-identical (same stimuli.json sha256, same figures, round quadruples, geometry, TERM = "DAX", seed, query conditions, parse, scoring), and re-measured.

Result (verbatim). Both models still recovered the most-recent binding at ceiling: SPONT latest-binding rate 1.000 (Wilson [0.926, 1.000]) for claude-sonnet-4.6 and gemini-3.5-flash, first-binding rate 0.000, physical-position-following at exactly chance (pick==last-line 0.250 / pick==first-line 0.250), DIRECT manipulation check 1.000 both directions, 0 NA, 160/160 strict parses (independent pre-run critic GO, independent post-run verifier REPRODUCED, 0 mismatches). "Numerically indistinguishable from Option A … removing the explicit flag changed nothing in the measured behaviour" (result/relational-implicit-reassignment-control, §"The headline"). So "the Option-A order-sensitivity is therefore not a surface artifact of the explicit reassignment wording" (§banner).

What it tightens. Bound (2) carried in the 2026-06-16 entry — "spontaneous" = query not recency-directed, not cue-free — is tightened to also flag-not-directed: the model is not told a reassignment occurred and "infers the multiplicity from the history and resolves it by recency" (result/relational-implicit-reassignment-control, §"What this shows"). Correspondingly, this essay's scoped picture leans on a sturdier Option-A regime: its recency-tracking is robust to dropping the explicit flag.

What it does NOT change. Everything else. Still a thin latest-binding-wins rule, the bottom rung — "This control removes a wording-artifact worry; it does not separate thin order-sensitivity from deep path-dependence" (result/relational-implicit-reassignment-control, §"What this shows"). Still order-sensitivity, not constitution — the strengthening must not drift toward constitution. Still anchor: internal-contrast-only — no human comparison ("none is owed because no human contrast is asserted," ibid.). Still not literally cue-free — the stamped history remains a cue; only the framing sentence was dropped. The essay's scoped, regime-dependent conclusion is unchanged; status stays revised.

2026-06-16 — triggers (a) and (b) both fired; thesis SCOPED to the v4 regime (status draft → revised)

Both pre-stated revision triggers fired in one session.
