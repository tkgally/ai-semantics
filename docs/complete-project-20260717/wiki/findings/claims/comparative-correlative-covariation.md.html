Claim: the decoder panel reliably tracks the comparative correlative's covariation structure and direction on the tested instruments

Statement

Across three replicated, controlled runs on the ratified 3-family panel (claude-sonnet-4.6, gpt-5.4-mini, gemini-3.5-flash), current instruction-tuned decoder LLMs reliably deploy the English comparative correlative's proportional-covariation reading on the tested items вҖ” they treat the more X, the more/less Y as licensing the inference that a change along the first scale goes with a fixed-direction change along the second, and they do so because of the construction, not the scalar vocabulary. The result is directional/ordering-level and robust across a graded battery of stress manipulations:

  • the inference is isolated to the construction: the panel asserts a covariation direction for ~100% of comparative-correlative (CC) items but for only 10вҖ“20% of matched non-CC controls that reuse the same words (v1 forced-choice gap +80.0 to +90.0 pp, 3/3 models вҖ” see Grounds, and the measurement caveat in Bounds);
  • it tracks direction, not a surface template: inverse CCs (the more X, the less Y) flip the asserted direction (v1 inverse-flip 95вҖ“100%, 3/3; v3 baseline-inv 100% on both instruments, 3/3);
  • it is not an n-gram/memorization effect: the inference persists at ~100% on deliberately absurd scale pairs whose direction can come only from the construction (v1 T3, within 0вҖ“4 pp of typical pairs, 3/3);
  • it follows the construction over conflicting world knowledge (v2 conflicting-cue: NLI 100% 3/3; forced-choice 100/83.3/83.3) and composes two-step covariation chains, including the diagnostic negativeГ—negativeвҶ’positive case a single-clause heuristic would fail (v2 multi-step 100%, both instruments, 3/3);
  • for 2 of 3 models it further survives an in-sentence operator that cancels or suspends the assertion вҖ” sentential negation and epistemic hedging (v3: claude-sonnet-4.6 and gemini-3.5-flash at/near ceiling on every embedded arm), so the robustness is not a bare "the-moreвҖҰthe-more вҶ’ increase" template.

On the single human-comparison arm the project has for this construction вҖ” the 30 real Scivetti CC NLI items, scored against the released per-item gold answer key and the aggregate вүҲ0.90 native-speaker baseline вҖ” the panel matches or slightly exceeds the human answer-key baseline (v1 NLI accuracy 93вҖ“100%). This human-comparison leg is narrow and single-run (see Human-comparison leg and Bounds).

This claim is deliberately scoped to a direction/ordering statement about performance on the tested instruments. It does not assert a precise magnitude with an interval (the runs are founding-N; a powered re-run is owed вҖ” Bounds), and it does not assert general human-level CC semantic competence or that the human-vs-model meaning gap is closed (What this claim does not say).

This is a genuine non-uniformity of the panel, carried explicitly in Bounds.

The controls that isolate the construction

The claim's "construction-driven, not lexical" force rests on the matched controls, not on the raw high rates: v1's T1 non-CC controls reuse the same scalar words in a non-CC syntax (the gap is the construction's contribution); v1's T2/baseline-inv inverse items guard against an increase-bias readout; v1's T3 absurd pairs guard against memorization; v2's conflicting-cue arm separates the construction from world knowledge; v2's multi-step composition separates real composition from last-clause matching; v3's in-sentence operators test compositional scope rather than extra-linguistic cues.

Human-comparison leg (narrow вҖ” v1 only, answer-key not gradient)

The only human anchor for this construction in-repo is the Scivetti CxNLI CC subset (resource/scivetti-2025-cxnli-dataset; ratified decisions/resolved/comparative-correlative-anchor). Inspection (2026-05-29) confirmed it delivers a single adjudicated gold label per item plus an aggregate вүҲ0.90 native-speaker baseline вҖ” an answer key, NOT a per-item multi-rater gradient. So the human-comparison statement here is answer-key agreement on 30 CC items on one run (v1): panel NLI accuracy 93вҖ“100% at or above вүҲ0.90. The v2/v3 hard arms (conflicting-cue, composition, negated/hedged CC) have no in-repo human norm and are internal-contrast-only by ratified decision (decisions/resolved/conflicting-cue-human-anchor); no human-comparison claim is made for them.

Where it sits on the evidence ladder

On the ladder in theory/constructional-meaning-in-llms (superseded s177 by theory/constructional-meaning-in-llms-v2 вҖ” cited here as the edition this page engaged.) this is a Tier-4 (inference-licensing) positive with a Tier-3 (atypical/generalization) control passed, stress-tested for conflicting-cue, multi-step composition, and operator scope. It is the project's most robust constructional positive and patterns with the theory's recurring observation that current decoders handle the add / easy direction of the upper ladder at ceiling. The theory page's standing caution travels with it unchanged: this is the "easy direction," and ceiling on a modest instrument bounds how deep a reading it licenses.

What this claim does NOT say

  • Magnitude (internal-contrast legs) now landed with intervals; still no human-comparison magnitude. The A2a powered re-run (result/comparative-correlative-covariation-powered, 2026-07-02, 136 fresh disjoint items, verifier-REPRODUCED) attaches the interval the founding runs could not: the construction-isolation assertion gap is вүҲ87 pp (86.8 / 88.2 / 86.8; 95% CIs excluding the 30pp gate and the null, lower bound вүҲ78 pp, 3/3 models), inverse-flip 97вҖ“100% and atypical-pair robustness (typвҲ’atyp вүӨ 5 pp) at/near ceiling with tight CIs. So the internal-contrast legs now state a magnitude+interval, not a threshold-pass. What is still not a magnitude: the human-comparison leg (the v1 Scivetti answer-key parity is single-run, 30 items, unchanged вҖ” the powered run made no human comparison), and the caveat below still binds вҖ” the near-ceiling rates give a precise magnitude, not a depth-of-processing verdict.
  • No general human-level CC competence claim. The human-comparison leg is a single-run, 30-item, answer-key agreement at ceiling вҖ” it cannot distinguish robust competence from task-easiness. "Reliably tracks CC covariation on the tested instruments" is not "understands comparative correlatives like humans."
  • The human-vs-model gap is not shown "closed." The v1 arm shows the panel matching the вүҲ0.90 answer-key baseline on 30 easy items; that contradicts the conjecture's "narrows-but-does-not-close" bet on this one instrument (see Bounds), but it is not a general closure claim вҖ” it is answer-key parity on one small ceiling arm.
  • Not uniform across the panel. gpt-5.4-mini cracks under forced-choice operator embedding (v3); the claim covers the panel including that crack, it does not paper over it.
  • No model-internal or grounding claim. Behavioral only; silent on representations and on grounding in Bender & Koller's sense (Tier-4 is the ceiling of text-internal evidence).

Bounds

  • Ceiling instrument (the lead caveat). Every run is at or near ceiling; when a task saturates it stops discriminating, so these results cannot separate "robust constructional competence" from "the task was easy for these models." This is the single most important limit and is why the claim is scoped to a directional/ordering statement on the tested instruments, not to a depth-of-processing verdict.
  • Founding-N in v1/v2/v3; the powered re-run has since landed. v1's constructed set is 20 items per cell; v2/v3 arms rest on 3вҖ“6 items each (a single item moves a v3 cell 25вҖ“33 pp). Under PROTOCOL.md В§4 a claim-carrying magnitude needs ~100вҖ“150 items вҖ” supplied by the A2a powered re-run (result/comparative-correlative-covariation-powered, 136 items, verifier-REPRODUCED): the construction-isolation, direction-flip, and atypical-robustness signatures reproduce on fresh disjoint items with 95% intervals (see the magnitude bullet above). The claim's direction/ordering scope is unchanged; the powered run firms it with intervals for the internal-contrast legs, and the ceiling caveat (next) still bounds the depth reading.
  • A measurement ambiguity in v1's T1. T1 clears 30 pp only under the assertion-rate reading of "CC-vs-control gap"; under the literal accuracy-difference reading it is ~10вҖ“17 pp and would not clear the threshold вҖ” the two diverge only because the controls are correctly answered at ceiling. The result page commits both quantities to its raw data and uses the assertion-rate reading (the design's stated purpose); the claim inherits that reading and flags it here rather than burying it.
  • gpt-5.4-mini FC over-inference under operator embedding. The panel is non-uniform at the operator-scope frontier (v3): the directional robustness holds for 2/3 models on both instruments, but gpt-5.4-mini's forced-choice reading of negated/hedged CCs is a specific excluded-middle over-inference. The claim's "for 2 of 3 models" scoping on the operator-embedding leg is load-bearing. (For symmetry: claude-sonnet-4.6 is not perfectly uniform either вҖ” its one embedded-arm slip is a single forced-choice miss on the hardest, doubly-recoded negi-alarm item, which the v3 page reads as noise rather than a mechanism; the "at/near ceiling" phrasing above covers it. The non-uniformity that is a mechanism is gpt-5.4-mini's.)
  • The conjecture's "narrows-but-not-closes" clause is contradicted on the v1 instrument. conjecture/comparative-correlative-construction bet the panel would stay measurably below the human baseline; on the v1 Scivetti arm it matched it вҖ” the conjecture's explicitly-flagged "stronger (closes)" outcome, on that one small ceiling instrument. This claim records that as answer-key parity on an easy arm, not as a general closure.
  • Single date, single panel, cross-model shared priors. Three decoder LLMs converging is weak evidence on its own; the Scivetti human anchor is what gives the v1 arm independent bearing, and it is small (30 items, answer-key). No temperature sweep beyond the two framings.
  • Cross-linguistic corroboration (s213 German, s215 Japanese) вҖ” a strengthening but still partial discharge of the English-n-gram worry. Two ports of the internal-contrast instrument (ratified scope decisions/resolved/cross-linguistic-cc-replication-scope, Q1-C/Q2-B/Q3-A) reproduce the construction-isolation gap, the inverse-flip, and the atypical-pair robustness on a different surface realization of the CC:
  • German (result/comparative-correlative-german-v1, 2026-07-12): gap +92.6/+88.2/+88.2 pp, inverse-flip 100/97/97%, atypical 95вҖ“100% on jeвҖҰdesto/umso (different function words, verb-final subordinate order). A modest lever вҖ” German is typologically close and its CC is itself frequent вҖ” so a partial discharge.
  • Japanese (result/comparative-correlative-japanese-v1, 2026-07-13): gap +94.1/+83.8/+95.6 pp (all CI lb вүҘ +75), inverse-flip 100/100/97%, atypical 100/95/95% on гҖңгҒ°гҖңгҒ»гҒ©. The stronger lever вҖ” typologically distant (SOV, agglutinative, verb-final) and, decisively, no overt comparative morpheme (no -er/more/mehr/desto; the "more" is carried by the гҒ°вҖҰгҒ»гҒ© frame + predicate repetition). So the reading is not English-theвҖҰthe-n-gram matching, nor even Germanic-template matching, since it reproduces where there is no comparative word at all. Both are within-model, internal-contrast-only replications (no non-English CC human dataset exists): they make no human comparison and no target-language-competence claim, and the reading did not track UD-treebank corpus frequency/co-occurrence in either. Two non-English languages beyond English is still not "all languages" вҖ” a strengthening, not a final closure. gpt-5.4-mini is again the weakest arm in Japanese (T1 +83.8; some control over-reading), consistent with the rest of the line. This claim's formal scope stays English; a cross-linguistic claim would need its own cross-session promotion review.

Anchor

This claim carries a resource anchors: link to resource/scivetti-2025-cxnli-dataset because it makes a (narrow) human-comparison statement вҖ” the v1 answer-key parity on the 30 real Scivetti CC items. That link grounds only the human-comparison leg. The internal-contrast legs (construction isolation, direction, n-gram robustness, conflicting-cue, composition, operator scope) draw their force from within-model contrasts and carry no human claim вҖ” consistent with the ratified internal-contrast-only status of the v2/v3 hard arms (decisions/resolved/conflicting-cue-human-anchor). The claim is therefore not marked anchor: internal-contrast-only: it does lean, in part, on the Scivetti human comparison, and states that leg at exactly its (single-run, answer-key, ceiling) strength.

Status

status: supported. What is supported is the scoped directional/ordering claim: the finding is replicated across three controlled runs on disjoint item sets, survives its matched controls and a graded stress battery, and carries a ratified human anchor on its (narrow) human-comparison leg. supported attaches to the direction and the construction-isolation, not to any magnitude and not to a general human-level-competence reading вҖ” both of which are explicitly disclaimed above. The three underlying results remain status: proposed (the promotion consolidates them; it does not restate their per-run readings). contingent-on: [] вҖ” every governing decision (anchor, operationalization, conflicting-cue anchor, v2 difficulty gate) is ratified.
